Audio Analysis

Methodology

Whether analyzing a campaign rally or a congressional debate, the tone of a speech is sometimes as important as the speech itself. When vocal pitch rises, it often indicates an activated emotional state. The audio analysis team employs speech-to-text software, as well as audio processing tools, to analyze the vocal inflections and intensity of emotion in politicians. The goal is to explore US congressional rhetoric from a different perspective.​​

Pitch is Fundamental

  • Whether you are analyzing a campaign rally or a congressional debate, sometimes the tone of a speech is just as important as the speech itself.​
  • When vocal pitch rises, it is often indicative of an activated emotional state caused by anger or fear. To determine whether the tone of speeches has changed over time, the audio analysis sub- team uses speech-to-text software alongside Python modules designed to extract audio features, like vocal pitch. ​
  • Ultimately, they hypothesize that vocal pitch is generally higher in speeches that were delivered recently, especially after the 2016 presidential election.​
